Inner ear anatomy

Spatial cues for dissection notes and imaging studies

Cochlea overview

Outline the turns of the porcine cochlea, preferred slicing planes, and reference images that show where common lesions emerge. Include tips for preserving Reissner’s membrane when processing fragile samples.

Vestibular map

Document utricle and saccule landmarks along with angles for endoscopic access. Add photos of intact crista ampullaris plus 3D scans for trainees learning to orient the semicircular canals.

Inner ear inflammation

Link clinical readouts with molecular drivers

Immune pathways

Summarize the cytokine panels we rely on (IL-1β, TNFα, IL-17) and how they map onto pig models of labyrinthitis. Note which assays require fresh tissue versus frozen homogenates.

Biomarkers

Track the emerging markers for fibrosis, nerve degeneration, and vascular leakage. Include references to prior publications and indicate where sample storage or handling could skew the results.

New member checklist

Everything to complete in the first month

Systems access

Confirm Slack, Benchling, Box, QReserve, and server credentials. Keep a running list of approvals (vivarium, imaging suite) so mentors can see progress.

Teach-back library

Each trainee records a short screencast explaining a concept learned from the primers above. Link your recording inside the Teach-back Airtable view so future members can binge-learn.
